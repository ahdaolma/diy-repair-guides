## Quick Diagnosis

| Symptom | Cause | Fix | Time | Cost |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Hums, no spin | Jammed flywheel | 1/4" hex key in bottom socket, rotate back/forth | 5 min | $2 |
| No sound at all | No power / thermal overload | Press red reset button on bottom, check GFCI/breaker | 10 min | $0-$3 |
| Water backs up in sink | Clogged P-trap beyond disposal | Boiling water → baking soda/vinegar → remove P-trap | 15-45 min | $0-$15 |
| Leaks from top | Sink flange seal failed | Replace plumber's putty seal | 30 min | $3 |
| Leaks from side | Dishwasher hose connection | Tighten clamp or replace rubber gasket | 10 min | $2 |
| Leaks from bottom | Internal seal failed | REPLACE UNIT (no repair possible) | 90 min | $80-$120 |
| Terrible smell | Rotting food under splash guard | Ice + rock salt scour, lemon, clean guard underside | 10 min | $5 |

---

## Hex Key Size

1/4-inch Allen wrench. Every hardware store: $2. If you lost the original disposal wrench, a standard 1/4-inch hex key works on ALL garbage disposals made since 1980.

---

## NEVER Put These in a Disposal

| Item | Why |
|---|---|
| Coffee grounds | Clump into sediment like cement in pipes |
| Eggshells | Membrane wraps around impellers |
| Potato peels | Starch + water = glue that coats pipes |
| Pasta/rice/bread | Expand in water, form hardening paste |
| Celery/asparagus/corn husks | Fibers wrap around impellers and jam flywheel |
| Grease/oil/fat | Congeals in pipes |
| Bones | Jam or damage shredder ring |
| Non-food items | Obvious. Use tongs to retrieve. UNPLUG FIRST. |

---

## Monthly Maintenance

- [ ] Ice cubes + 1/4 cup rock salt, run 30 seconds with cold water (scours shredder ring)
- [ ] Half lemon (quartered, peel on), run 15 seconds (citric acid + deodorizing)
- [ ] Peel back rubber splash guard, scrub underside with dish brush + hot soapy water
- [ ] Check under sink for leaks while disposal runs
